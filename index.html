Background

Customers visiting Retail Outlets put down reviews on multiple platforms. Customer reviews often contain granular information regarding facilities, service quality, cleanliness etc.

Opportunity

Aspect-based automated scoring of reviews can provide insights into RO performance on specific metrics like facilities, service, housekeeping, fuel quality, location, etc. Comparative analysis of ROs with nearby competing ROs can provide insights into building competitive advantage

Solution

We shortlisted critical aspects and associated keywords for scoring. Then, we developed a Natural Language Processing-based sentiment analysis algorithm for analyzing reviews. Finally, we operationalised the model via interactive dashboard.

Google provides a user-friendly platform for customers to leave reviews and share their experiences with various businesses, including ROs. For this particular initiative, around 14,000 reviews have been collected and analyzed using various tools and techniques to gain insights into customers' opinions and feedback on different aspects of the ROs, such as facilities, service quality, cleanliness, location, and fuel quality. These insights can help ROs to improve their services and facilities, address customer concerns, and stay competitive in the market.
